How to Make a Vase out of a Plastic Bottle

Created by Philipp A. Magsaysay

Last Updated: October 20, 2023

Description:

Creating a vase from a plastic bottle is a fun and eco-friendly DIY project.

You will need:

Plastic Bottle

Scissors/Cutter

Any decoration of your choice

• Fold the Bottle into your Desired Design

STEP

1

Cut the top off any size plastic bottle.

Cut the top off any size plastic bottle just below where it curves inward. Most plastic bottles curve inward
about a third of the way up, making a triangle for the cap to go on top of. Find where the bottle starts curving and
place the tip of a utility knife just below that. Then cut around the bottle in a straight line until you come back to
the starting point and the top of the bottle detaches.

• Try to keep the cut as level as possible, but don’t worry if you curve a little bit. You can even out the line in the next step.

• If you need a smaller vase, you can also cut the bottle at the lower point.

• Be very careful cutting the bottle. Keep your fingers away from the blade and always cut away from your body.

STEP

2

Even out the top of the cut with scissors.

Make the top of the cut as even as possible. Use scissors and smooth out any uneven spots by snipping them
off. Work around the bottle perimeter until the edge is even.

• The edges don’t have to be perfectly even. Just avoid any large bulges or curves that are out of line with the rest
the border.

• Decorate the Plastic Bottle

STEP

3

Draw a design along the edge of the bottle with a marker.

Paint the bottle a color of your choosing. The best paints for painting on plastic are acrylic or spray paint.
Choose one of these types and pick a color for your vase. If you want, you can also use multiple colors on the
vase. Alternatively, leave some sections clear and paint in others for a nice effect. You have lots of options for
painting your vase.[12]

• If you spray paint, make sure to do it in an open area and cover anything that could get paint on it.

• The bottle may need 2 coats of paint. Apply the first coat and let it dry. If you can still see the plastic through
the paint, then add another coat

Place rhinestones or stickers around the bottle for extra decoration. If you want more decoration, these are great
options. Apply hot glue to rhinestones and stick them around the bottle. You can also use decorative stickers for a
simpler approach.

• This is a great activity for kids. Give them a pack of stickers and let them decorate the vase however they want.
Since it's plastic, you don't have to worry about them breaking it.
